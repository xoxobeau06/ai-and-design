What it is

What it is not

It is not a final style guide, a bloated component library, a random template pack, or a purely theoretical essay. It is scaffolding.

03

The AI Role

AI acts as a pattern amplifier and variation engine. You retain the human responsibility for constraints, taste, and final decisions.

Why Systems Matter

There is a profound difference between a moodboard, a style tile, and a visual system.

  • Consistency & Repetition: Without a system, interfaces fray at the edges. Systems establish the rhythm.
  • The 50 Shades Trap: Avoid creating too many primitive tokens without semantic meaning.
  • The Component Hoarder: Building variants without defining typography logic leads to chaos.

The 5 Core Components

The Figma toolkit is structured into five distinct phases of system thinking.

1

System Comparison Template

Structured comparison frames with columns for color logic, typography scale, spacing, and tone. Extract rules, don't just copy aesthetics.

2

Reference Annotation

Categorized inspiration frames with required annotation tags. Explain why the reference works using X-Ray vision measuring tools.

3

The Constraint Contract

Define 2–3 core constraints. What is the rationale? What does it allow? What does it prevent? Stress-test them with extreme exercises.

4

AI Prompt Dashboard

Mad-libs style prompt templates for structured variation. Know explicitly when to use AI and when human taste must prevail.

5

Token Translator Workshop

Primitive color and semantic tokens mapping. Unstyled core components waiting for your constraints to breathe life into them.

The Human-AI-Human Loop

AI is a phenomenal permutation engine, but a terrible author. Learn the protocol for integration without abdication.

1. Human (Context & Constraints)

Define the rigid boundaries (e.g., "Only Inter, 3 weights, 4-step spacing").

2. AI (Permutation & Variation)

Prompting the AI to explore within the boundaries to generate bulk CSS or layout tests.

3. Human (Curation & Refinement)

Select the best outcome, check for system adherence, and apply stylistic taste.
